## 2026-03-01 - [Dashboard Readability & Spawning Feedback]
**Learning:** Visual indicators like progress bars for temporal actions (spawning) and permanent stats (RCL) significantly reduce the cognitive load of monitoring system state. In busy environments (like a Screeps map), a semi-transparent background for dashboard overlays is crucial for readability regardless of the underlying terrain.
**Action:** Always include high-contrast backgrounds for UI text overlays and use progress bars for any action with a predictable duration.
